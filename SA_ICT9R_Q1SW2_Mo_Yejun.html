Crisostomo Ibarra Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin is a tall, goodlooking, and a well mannered male character from a wealthy family.
Capitan Tiago or Kapitan Tiago Capitan Tiago is a male character who is husband to Dona Pia Alba and father to Maria Clara. Because of his contributions aof financial support, he became close with the priests.
Maria Clara Mara Clara is recognized as the most stunning and well-known woman in San Diego.
Dona Pia Alba Dona Pia Alba is a female character who is wife to Capitan Tiago and mother to Maria Clara . Shortly after giving birth to Maria Clara, Dona Pia Alba passed away.
Padre Damaso Despite being a priest, he has a poor character and is corrupt. Even after spending a lot of time listening to confessions from Filipinos, he is still condescending and lacks fundamental language abilities.
